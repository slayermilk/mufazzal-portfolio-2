About Me

Hi! I'm Mufazzal, an aspiring photographer with a passion for capturing the beauty of everyday moments. Whether it's the raw emotion of a candid shot or the intricate details in nature, I love telling stories through my lens. My journey in photography started as a simple hobby but has grown into a creative outlet where I explore various styles and techniques. Through my work, I aim to evoke feelings, inspire thought, and create lasting memories. I'm constantly learning, experimenting, and pushing the boundaries of my art to bring fresh perspectives to my audience. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to collaborate or just chat about photography!

I shoot with a Nikon D3100, a reliable camera that's been with me throughout my photography journey. For versatility, I use two lenses: a 50mm f/1.8, perfect for capturing portraits and low-light scenes, and an 18-200mm lens, which gives me flexibility for both wide-angle and zoom shots. I also keep things steady with a sturdy tripod and a clamp, essential tools for getting those sharp, well-composed images.
